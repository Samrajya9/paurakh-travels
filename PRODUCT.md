# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Adventure travelers and trekkers planning trips to Nepal — often researching from home before booking a guided expedition. They need inspiration, trust, and a fast path to the right trek or tour package.

## Product Purpose

Paurakh Travels is a Nepal-based travel company offering guided treks, cultural tours, and custom journeys. The marketing site must communicate Himalayan expertise, inspire confidence, and route visitors toward package discovery and booking.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, adventurous, trustworthy — the voice of experienced local guides who know the mountains intimately. Evokes altitude, clarity, and the quiet awe of high places without resorting to generic adventure clichés.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS landing pages with metric heroes, cream/beige backgrounds, identical icon-card grids, and stock-photo montages. Avoid editorial-magazine serif aesthetics and overused trekking tropes (tiny uppercase section labels, numbered step scaffolding).

## Design Principles

1. Let real mountain imagery carry the emotional weight — photography is the design.
2. Make the path to finding a trek obvious from the first screen.
3. Show local expertise through specific place names and authentic copy, not vague superlatives.
4. Commit to the brand orange accent sparingly but decisively on primary actions.
5. Respect the altitude: calm hierarchy, readable contrast on photographic backgrounds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Ensure text on image overlays meets contrast requirements. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for any entrance animations.
